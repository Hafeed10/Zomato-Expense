# Food Stats - Zomato Food Expense Tracker 🍛


**Food Stats** is a Chrome extension that gives you deep insights into your Zomato ordering habits. It analyzes your order history to provide a beautiful, privacy-first dashboard showing your total spending, top restaurants, and ordering trends.

> **Live Extension:** [Available on Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/food-stats/ojmpicpmgjgjhlckcmfgcheeekpaikka)  

---

## ✨ Features

- **📊 Lifetime Expense Tracker**: Instantly see your "Total Spent", "Total Orders", and "Average Order Value".
- **📈 Visual Spending Trends**: Interactive line graph showing your monthly spending history.
- **🕒 Order Time Analysis**: Discover your peak ordering times (lunch vs. dinner vs. late-night) with a detailed bar chart.
- **🏆 Top Restaurants**: A ranked list of your top 10 most-ordered restaurants by spend and frequency.
- **🍩 Category & Restaurant Breakdown**: Doughnut chart visualizing your spending distribution among your favorite spots.
- **📅 Flexible Filtering**: Analyze data by "Last 30 Days", "Last 3 Months", "Last 1 Year", or "All Time".
- **🔒 Privacy First**: Your data belongs to you. All processing happens locally in your browser, and no data is ever sent to external servers.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Core**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla)
- **Visualization**: Chart.js for interactive graphs
- **Platform**: Chrome Extension Manifest V3

## 🚀 Installation (Local Development)

If you want to run this extension locally or contribute to the code:

1. **Clone the Repository**
   ```bash
   https://github.com/Hafeed10/Zomato-Expense.git
   cd Food-stats-extenstion
   ```

2. **Load into Chrome**
   - Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
   - Toggle **Developer mode** in the top-right corner.
   - Click **Load unpacked**.
   - Select the project directory you just cloned.

3. **Pin the Extension**
   - Click the puzzle piece icon in your Chrome toolbar.
   - Pin **Food Stats** for easy access.

---

## 📖 How to Use

1. **Log in to Zomato**: Ensure you are logged into your account on [zomato.com](https://www.zomato.com).
2. **Open the Extension**: Click the **Food Stats** icon in your toolbar.
3. **Sync Data**: Click the **"Sync Data"** button. The extension will open Zomato (if not already open) and safely fetch your order history.
4. **Explore Insights**: Once the sync is complete, the dashboard will populate with your personalized stats and charts.
